  • To send out recommendations to your current IT concerns you had inquired
  • To provide services on the products and services you had inquired.
  • To distribute marketing content on the products and services offered by you

This privacy policy will be reviewed and periodically updated as we grow and expand our business services.

If you have questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by sending your queries and concerns to info@iforcetek.com
