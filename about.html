Vision

We are the trusted, valued and preferred technology partners for local small business and enterprises that need web and mobile technology expertise provided as a solution to manage and grow their products and services on the web.

Culture

Our corporate culture emphasizes open communication, encourages self-empowerment, recognizes and retains talent and high performance, provide opportunity to learn and enhance from success and failures, and provide a unified platform to draw from knowledge share.

Organizational Values
  • Quality First
  • Cost Effective Solution
  • Derive Clarify out of Chaos
  • Simplistic Approach
  • Social Consciousness
  • Responsibility and Accountability
Women Empowerment

As a women owned business we are ardent supporters of women in business and empower them by advocating the usability of technology tools. We encourage and hire women interns and volunteers, train them on technology products and services by providing hands on access to gain expertise on end-user scenario.

Mission

We work with the customer to understand their business vision, budget, requirement needs and provide a scalable and affordable solution and guide them to take their digital service, products and business efficiency to the next level.

Strategy

We blend the right mix of culture, strategy and business efficiency approach in our services that meet or exceed customer needs. Iforcetek partners with allied technology product and service providers to offer a seamless technology solution for our clients.

Capabilities Statement

We offer technology solutions in the areas of responsive web app development, mobile app for Android, iOS and Windows platform, app customization and integration.
We assist non-profit, membership organizations and clubs website integrated and automated membership management that offer seamless content access to members and visitors.
We offer Beta product release to startup ideas on a budget that enables business owners to test the market with a viable product and scale them as client adoption increases.
We offer Small Business Web Package that includes online client engagement, sell products and services online, stream live and hosted events online and derive maximum value from online business presence.
