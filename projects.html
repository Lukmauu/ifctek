Line of Business App Mash up

LOB mash ups enable critical data flow between line of business apps like CRM, Sales and Order Tracking, Inventory, Accounting, Invoicing and Payment Systems that reduces re-entry of data into these various applications. The following sequence captures a typical data flow for a business model:

  1. Prospective customer information stored into CRM.
  2. Periodical sale of products/service promotions sent to prospective leads.
  3. Periodical events are generated and distributed to prospective leads.
  4. Prospective sale order closure.
  5. Customer visits online portal.
  6. Shopping cart of products and services listed in website with payment gateway.
  7. Customer purchases product/service via online portal.
  8. Invoice generated and payment accepted on online orders.
  9. Invoice posted to accounting system.
  10. Periodical Budgets, Performance and Projection Reports generated.

Most small business use standalone software tools and apps for these various lines of business activities and often re-enter data over and over to get their critical business data reflected on all these systems which arises data inconsistency due to errors in data entry. Our mash up service enables users to connect with multiple applications and allows cross communication between platform independent apps thereby reducing redundant data entry.

Integrating Social Media Tools with Business Website

Social media marketing and online content has become an integral part of business growth. Social media collaboration tools like blog, newsletter and email blast, online content sharing like video, text and pictures are now a robust tool to promote and grow business. It is a smart step for small business to adopt social media tools and channels like twitter, Facebook, Google+ to promote and share business content to sell products and services online. With availability of open source platform it is now getting popular to integrate these social media tools to your business website and share & distribute content across these digital media from the business website. Our social media tool integration services will connect the business website with WordPress blog sites, Facebook, twitter, and LinkedIn. Feeds and tweets can be posted to the business site to showcase twitter, Facebook activity.

Web & Mobile app for Service Alert

This is a line of business application for companies that operate in service sectors like banks, restaurants, data centers, cloud computing and hosted services and for those businesses that runs mission critical applications which impacts business operations and customer interfaces on a day to day basis. This app sends an SMS alert to the mobile phones of those contacts captured in the notification database if there is an interruption to the services offered along with a possible ETA. The web service part of the app provides ability to select an alert code based on the severity of the alert and provides interface to capture pertinent and specific information about the alert in terms of location, service specialty and compiles the message as SMS text and sends it as an alert to mobile phone contacts.

Business Card Filer Mobile App

Content marketing platform is shrinking into hand held devices like mobile phone, notepad etc. The need for businesses to access content in their mobile phone is becoming significantly important. With this growing trend, the contact management and content marketing has become a much sought after service to promote business via mobile phones. With this in mind we are developing a mobile application that scans business cards and stores them as image files locally on mobile phones and also stores them on a hosted server and enables the user to synch up the contact database and access it on the go from anywhere in the globe. The app scans pertinent data from the image file like Name, mobile number, email id and create/update the contact card locally on the mobile phone and on the server.
